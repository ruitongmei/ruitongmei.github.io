## About

<p class="lead">I am <strong>Ruitong Mei</strong> (梅睿桐), a master's student in the <a href="https://github.com/SUDA-LA">SUDA-HLT</a> team at <a href="https://www.suda.edu.cn/">Soochow University</a>, advised by <a href="https://web.suda.edu.cn/zhli13/">Prof. Zhenghua Li</a>.</p>

My current research focuses on natural language processing, especially Chinese word segmentation, dependency parsing, large language models. I am interested in building reliable linguistic resources and using large language models for annotation checking, feedback-driven data revision, and low-resource NLP data augmentation.

<div class="research-snapshot">
  <div>
    <span class="snapshot-label">Current Focus</span>
    <strong>Reliable Chinese word segmentation data</strong>
    <p>Detecting local boundary inconsistencies and building efficient revision workflows with model signals, LLM judgments, and human review.</p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <span class="snapshot-label">Methods</span>
    <strong>Annotation refinement</strong>
    <p>Combining probability-based candidates, multi-granularity standards, and reviewer feedback to improve gold data quality.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="topic-list" aria-label="Research topics">
